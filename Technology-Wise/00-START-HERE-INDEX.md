## 🎓 INTERVIEW TIPS

### **BEFORE INTERVIEW:**
- Read all materials once (overview)
- Get 8 hours sleep
- Mock interview morning of
- Eat healthy breakfast
- Review STAR stories

### **DURING INTERVIEW:**
- Listen carefully, don't interrupt
- Think out loud (show reasoning)
- Draw diagrams for system design
- Admit if you don't know (honest > wrong)
- Ask clarifying questions
- Provide code examples

### **AFTER INTERVIEW:**
- Note what you couldn't answer
- Study those topics tonight
- Thank interviewer (email)
- Don't over-analyze (you did great!)
