# Leadership and Behavioral (Senior)

## Must-practice questions
1. Tell me about yourself.
2. Why this role now?
3. Tough production incident.
4. Conflict with cross-team partner.
5. Mentoring junior engineers.
6. Handling changing requirements.
7. Delivery under tight deadlines.
8. Stakeholder management.
9. Technical decision with trade-offs.
10. Biggest failure and learning.

## 30-second answer template
- Situation
- Responsibility
- Action
- Measurable result

## Senior phrases to use
- "I optimized for business impact and risk reduction."
- "I made the trade-offs explicit to stakeholders."
- "I balanced delivery speed with operational safety."

## Must-not-miss points
- Mention measurable impact
- Mention team/cross-team collaboration
- Mention what you changed in process after learning
