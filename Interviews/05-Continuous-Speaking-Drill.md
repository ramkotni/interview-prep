# Continuous Speaking Drill (No-Gap Interview Practice)

Goal: Speak clearly for 30 sec, 60 sec, and 120 sec on any question.

## 1) Universal answer frame
- **C:** Context
- **P:** Problem
- **A:** Action
- **N:** NFR focus
- **R:** Result

Say this mentally: "C-P-A-N-R"

## 2) 30-second answer template
"In [project/domain], we faced [problem]. I handled it by [core action]. I focused on [NFR: latency/security/reliability]. As a result, [measurable outcome]."

## 3) 60-second answer template
1. Business context (10 sec)
2. Technical issue (10 sec)
3. Actions in sequence (25 sec)
4. NFR and impact (15 sec)

## 4) 120-second answer template
1. Architecture context
2. Incident/problem details
3. Immediate containment
4. Root cause fix
5. Long-term preventive controls
6. Outcome with numbers

## 5) Bridge phrases (use when you blank out)
- "Let me structure this clearly."
- "I will explain this in three parts."
- "First containment, second root cause, third prevention."
- "From a reliability perspective, here is what changed."

## 6) Fillers to avoid and replacements
- Avoid: "Actually... basically..."
- Use: "Specifically..."
- Avoid: "It depends..."
- Use: "The choice depends on scale, consistency, and cost; in this case I chose..."

## 7) 10-minute daily drill
1. Pick one leadership Q
2. Pick one Java/Spring Q
3. Pick one system design Q
4. Pick one project STAR story
5. Record and listen once

## 8) Scoring rubric (self-check)
Score each 1-5:
- Clarity
- Structure
- Technical depth
- NFR mention
- Business impact

## 9) Final 20-second close in interviews
"I can drive architecture and implementation hands-on, stabilize production quickly, and deliver secure, scalable systems with strong business impact."
